Deliverable D0 is produced by Step 0 and is essentially the abstract idea of the autonomous system. It captures a high-level concept for a railway system.

The level of detail to which D0 is produced will determine the difficulty in producing future deliverables. D0 can range from:

**"A fully autonomous System going from X to Y"**

To a more specific concept such as:

**"A metro system equipped with Lidar, Radar and head-mounted cameras, operating at, or close to GoA-4, supplemented by X,Y,Z, travelling from station A to B, without human intervention."**

A recommended format for D0 is a series of scoping statements, such as:

• Area of Operation

• Primary Function of Autonomy

• Authority Allocation

• Ecosystem dependencies and interfaces

• Assumptions

With each being filled for the respective route.
